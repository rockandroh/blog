Predictive Data Minining Model Intro

Knowledge discovery involves finding interesting patterns from data stored in large databases through use of computer analysis.

  • Interesting implies non-trivial, implicit, previously unknown, easily understood, useful and actionable knowledge.

  • Information is defined as the pattersn, correlations, rules, or relationships in data providing knowledge useful in decision making.

  • Descriptive analytics

    • reports of what has happened. Statistics are big part of that.

  • Predictive analytics

    • extend statistical and/or aritifical intelligence to provide forecasting capability.

  • Diagnostic analytics

    • apply analysis to sensor input to direct control systems automatically

Data mining includes descriptive and predictive modeling. Operations research includes all three.
